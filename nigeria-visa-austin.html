Nigeria e-Visa — The Fastest Option for Austin Travelers

Nigeria's electronic visa (e-Visa) system allows eligible U.S. citizens to apply for a Nigerian visa entirely online, without visiting a consulate in person. This is the fastest and most convenient route for most Austin residents traveling to Nigeria for tourism or short business visits.

How the Nigeria e-Visa Works

The Nigeria e-Visa is applied for through the Nigeria Immigration Service (NIS) official portal. The process works as follows:

  1. Create an account on the Nigeria Immigration Service e-Visa portal
  2. Complete the online application form accurately — errors are a common cause of rejection
  3. Upload required documents (passport scan, photo, flight itinerary, invitation letter if applicable)
  4. Pay the e-Visa fee online
  5. Receive your e-Visa approval by email within 48–72 hours
  6. Print your e-Visa and carry it with your passport when traveling

Austin Passport Pros handles steps 1–5 on your behalf, ensuring every field is completed correctly and every required document is uploaded in the right format. A single error in the application can cause delays or rejection, which is especially stressful when you have flights booked.

Nigeria e-Visa Types

  • Tourist e-Visa: For leisure travel, visiting family or friends in Nigeria. Typically valid for 30–90 days single entry.
  • Business e-Visa: For attending meetings, conferences, trade events, or short-term business activities in Nigeria. Requires an invitation letter from a Nigerian business or organization.
  • Temporary Work Permit (TWP): For short-term employment or contract work in Nigeria. Requires sponsorship from a Nigerian employer.

Nigeria Embassy Visa — Houston Consulate for Austin Residents

Certain Nigeria visa categories and longer-term stays require an in-person or mail-in application to the Nigerian Consulate General in Houston, TX — the nearest Nigerian consulate to Austin. Categories that may require the embassy route include:

  • Long-term work visa (Subject to Regularization / STR visa)
  • Diplomatic or official travel
  • Journalists and media personnel
  • Applicants whose e-Visa was denied and who need to appeal or reapply

Austin Passport Pros prepares your complete application package for Houston consulate submission — correctly completed forms, properly formatted photos, organized supporting documents — and guides you through the submission process from start to finish.

Documents Required for a Nigeria Visa from Austin

Nigeria e-Visa Requirements

  • Valid U.S. passport with at least 6 months of validity beyond your travel dates and at least one blank page
  • Digital passport photo — white background, clear, recent, meeting Nigerian immigration specifications
  • Confirmed return flight itinerary (not required to be a paid ticket, but must show entry and exit dates)
  • Hotel booking confirmation or a letter of invitation from a host in Nigeria (name, address, phone number, relationship to applicant)
  • For business e-Visa: official invitation letter from a Nigerian company on letterhead, including the company's registration number
  • e-Visa application fee (paid online by credit/debit card)

Nigeria Embassy Visa Requirements (Houston Consulate)

  • Completed Nigerian visa application form (printed and signed)
  • Valid U.S. passport with 6+ months validity and at least 2 blank pages
  • Two recent passport-sized photos meeting Nigerian consular specifications
  • Return flight itinerary
  • Hotel reservation or letter of invitation from Nigerian host
  • Yellow fever vaccination certificate (required for entry into Nigeria)
  • For business: company invitation letter and business registration documents
  • Consular application and service fees

Important: Nigeria requires proof of yellow fever vaccination for all travelers. Make sure your yellow fever vaccine is up to date and that you carry your International Certificate of Vaccination (yellow card). Austin Passport Pros reminds you of all health requirements when preparing your application.
