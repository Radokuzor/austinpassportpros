How long does passport renewal take in Austin, TX?

Can I get a same-day passport in Austin, TX?

Yes — with documented travel within 72 hours and the right documents, you may qualify for a same-day emergency passport appointment at the nearest regional passport agency. Call us immediately and we'll guide you through every step.

What documents do I need for a passport application in Austin?

For a new passport: completed DS-11 form, proof of U.S. citizenship, valid government-issued photo ID, a photocopy of your ID, one 2×2 passport photo, and applicable fees. For renewal (DS-82): your most recent passport, a passport photo, and fees. We help you prepare everything in advance.
