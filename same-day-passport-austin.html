Do You Qualify for a Same-Day Emergency Passport?

Emergency same-day passport service through the U.S. State Department is reserved for travelers with urgent, documented needs. You may qualify if:

  • You have international travel within 72 hours (3 days) and can provide proof (flight itinerary, booking confirmation)
  • You have a life-or-death emergency abroad involving a close family member (parent, child, sibling, spouse) and can provide supporting documentation
  • You need to travel for urgent humanitarian reasons with appropriate documentation

If your travel is within 2–4 weeks rather than 72 hours, you may qualify for expedited processing (2–3 weeks) through a regional passport agency appointment rather than true same-day service. Austin Passport Pros assesses your specific situation and recommends the fastest available path.

Documents Required for an Emergency Same-Day Passport

The exact documents required depend on whether you are applying for a new passport, renewing, or replacing a lost/stolen passport. Generally you will need:

  • Completed application form (DS-11 for new, DS-82 for renewal, DS-64 for lost/stolen)
  • Proof of U.S. citizenship (original birth certificate or naturalization certificate)
  • Valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license, state ID)
  • Photocopy of your photo ID (front and back on one page)
  • Two passport photos (2×2 inches, taken within 6 months)
  • Proof of urgent travel (flight itinerary, hotel confirmation, or medical/emergency documentation)
  • Applicable government fees

Missing even one document can result in your appointment being turned away. Austin Passport Pros prepares you thoroughly so you arrive with a complete, correct package.
