U.S. Passport Photo Requirements

The U.S. State Department requires the following for all passport photos:

  • Size: Exactly 2×2 inches (51×51 mm)
  • Recency: Taken within the last 6 months to reflect your current appearance
  • Background: Plain white or off-white only — no patterns, gradients, or shadows
  • Color: Must be a color photo — black and white photos are not accepted
  • Facial expression: Neutral expression or natural smile, with both eyes open
  • Head position: Full face, front view — head must not be tilted or turned
  • Head size: The head must measure between 1 inch and 1⅛ inches from bottom of chin to top of head
  • Glasses: Not permitted in passport photos (effective November 2016)
  • Head coverings: Only permitted for religious purposes, and must not obscure the face
  • Print quality: High resolution, no pixelation, no shadows across the face

Common Passport Photo Mistakes That Cause Rejections

These are the most frequent reasons passport photos are rejected by the State Department:

  • Shadows on the face or background caused by poor lighting
  • Background that isn't pure white or off-white (cream walls, gray walls, and other tints often fail)
  • Head size too small or too large within the frame
  • Wearing glasses (no longer permitted)
  • Photo taken more than 6 months ago
  • Mouth open or exaggerated expression
  • Red-eye or other digital photo artifacts
  • Photo printed at incorrect size or resolution

A rejected photo doesn't just cause frustration — it delays your entire passport application, which can be a serious problem if you have upcoming travel. Getting your photo done professionally eliminates this risk.

Digital Passport Photos vs. Printed Photos

As of 2024, the U.S. State Department accepts digital passport photo uploads for passport applications submitted online. However, the same technical specifications still apply — the photo must meet all size, background, expression, and quality requirements. If you're submitting a paper application, you'll need printed 2×2 photos.
